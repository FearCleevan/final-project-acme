## What I Accomplished Today (06/03/2026):

**1. Signs Page — Scott's 3 Sign Products Now Live**
Scott sent over photos and details of 3 antique enamel advertising signs (Cadbury's, James Hinks & Sons, Veritas Mantles) at $890 each. Set up everything needed to display them on the site:

Scott's direction was simply "Signs" — not "Advertising Signs." Updated every place it appeared: the top navigation, sidebar filter, mobile menu, footer links, and the page title.

**3. Admin Dashboard — Product Form Replaced Modal Popup with Full Pages**
The Add Product and Edit Product popup windows had two persistent issues — the background page kept scrolling behind the popup, and the popup itself could not be scrolled with the mouse wheel. After multiple failed attempts to fix the popup scroll behavior, replaced the modals entirely with dedicated full pages:
- Clicking Add Product now goes to `/admin/products/new` (its own full page)
- Clicking any product row now goes to `/admin/products/[id]` (its own full page)
- Both pages have a breadcrumb at the top, sticky Save/Discard buttons, and auto-redirect back to the products list after saving
- Added draft auto-save — if the browser refreshes mid-form on a new product, all typed fields are restored automatically

**4. Fixed Two Vercel Deployment Build Errors**
- First error: New Product and Edit Product pages use a Next.js hook (`useSearchParams`) that requires being wrapped in a `<Suspense>` container during static build — added this, build passed
- Second error: Changed Shopify data fetching from "no cache" (which blocked static page generation) back to a 5-minute refresh window — build passed, new products appear on the site within 5 minutes of being added to Shopify

**5. Small Fixes**
- Fixed the breadcrumb/actions bar covering the admin header on the new product pages
- Fixed a linter that kept silently changing `'signs'` to `'sign'` (missing the s) in the catalog sidebar filter, causing the Signs filter to return zero results
