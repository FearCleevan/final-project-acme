## What Got Done Today

### 1. Email Preview — Now Works Like a Real Email Tool

Before today, the only way to see what your email looked like was a tiny toggle that showed a preview crammed into the page. Not great.

Now it works like Mailchimp or Klaviyo — you click **"Preview"** and a proper full-screen popup appears. Inside you can flip between **Desktop** (how it looks on a computer) and **Mobile** (how it looks on a phone). If it looks good, you hit **"Looks good — Send Now"** right from that popup. Press Escape or click outside to go back to editing.

Also fixed a security issue where a bad actor who somehow got into the admin could inject code into the preview. All user-typed content is now safely escaped before being displayed.
