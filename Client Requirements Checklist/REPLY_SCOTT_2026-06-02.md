Yes, they do charge a small processing fee on each sale — typically around 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (similar to PayPal or any online payment processor). If you use **Shopify Payments** (their own built-in payment system), they waive the extra platform fee, so you only pay the standard card processing rate. For a store your size, Shopify Payments is the most cost-effective option and the one we recommend.

There are alternatives (like WooCommerce), but they require a lot more technical setup and maintenance on your end — not worth the hassle for what you need.
