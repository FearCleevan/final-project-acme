## Tips for Taking Photos

You don't need a professional camera — a smartphone works perfectly. Here are a few quick tips:

- Place the item on a **white sheet, white cardboard, or white table**
- Shoot in **natural daylight** near a window (avoid flash if possible)
- Make sure the item is **fully in frame** with a little space around the edges
- Take the photo from **straight on** or slightly above — avoid extreme angles
