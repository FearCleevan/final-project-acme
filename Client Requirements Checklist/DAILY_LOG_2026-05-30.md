## What I Accomplished Today

### 1. Shopping Cart — Connected to Real Shopify

Up until today, the shopping cart ("Your Crate") was only saving items locally in the browser. When a customer closed the tab or came back later, their cart could be out of sync with Shopify. Today, that was fully fixed.

**What was built:**

- The cart is now directly connected to Shopify behind the scenes. When a customer adds a product, updates a quantity, or removes an item, the site quietly tells Shopify in real time — so everything stays in sync.
- The cart is saved to the customer's browser so it survives page refreshes and tab closures.
- When a customer returns to the site, the cart automatically reconnects to their existing Shopify cart and restores everything exactly as they left it.
- Each product in the cart is now properly linked to its exact Shopify item — which is required for checkout to work correctly.
- All of this happens invisibly in the background. From the customer's perspective, the cart just feels instant and reliable.

### 2. Cart Bug Fixes — Quantity & Error Issues

During testing after the Shopify connection was set up, a few bugs were found and resolved.

**Bug 1 — Customers could add more items than what's in stock**
- What happened: The quantity limit on the product detail page was working, but clicking "Add to Crate" repeatedly from the catalog bypassed it — a customer could end up with more in their cart than physically exists.
- Fixed: The stock limit is now enforced everywhere, no matter how the item is added. If a product has 3 in stock, the cart will never go above 3.

**Bug 2 — Clicking the + button rapidly was causing errors**
- What happened: Tapping or clicking the + button quickly several times sent multiple requests to Shopify at the same time. Shopify rejected the overlapping requests with a conflict error and logged it in the browser console.
- Fixed: The site now waits a brief moment after the last click before sending anything to Shopify. The number on screen updates instantly with every tap, but only one clean request goes to Shopify once the customer stops clicking.

**Bug 3 — Old cart data was causing repeated errors**
- What happened: If the cart connection to Shopify went stale (e.g., the cart expired), the site kept trying to update it using the old, invalid reference — triggering the same error over and over.
- Fixed: The moment a sync attempt fails, the site clears the outdated reference and immediately reconnects to Shopify to get the correct cart data. Errors no longer repeat.

**Bug 4 — + button was not capping at stock limit on the full cart page**
- What happened: The cart drawer (the slide-out panel) correctly disabled the + button at the stock limit, but the full `/crate` page did not — a customer could keep tapping + indefinitely on that page.
- Fixed: The stock limit is now enforced on the full cart page as well. The + button greys out and becomes untappable once the max quantity is reached, consistent across the whole site.

### 3. Product Page — Quantity Now Stays in Sync with the Cart

**What the problem was:** If a customer added the same product 10 times from the catalog (e.g., tapping "Add to Crate" on the listing cards), the cart would correctly show a quantity of 10. But when that customer clicked through to the product's detail page, the quantity selector on that page still showed "1" — as if the cart were empty. Tapping "Add to Crate" from there would then stack even more on top of the existing 10.

**What was fixed:**
- The quantity selector on the product detail page now reads the cart first when it loads. If you already have 4 of that item in your cart, the selector opens at 4 — not 1.
- The selector stays in sync in real time. If you change the quantity in the cart drawer while the product page is open, the product page updates to match.
- The "Add to Crate" button now behaves differently depending on whether the item is already in the cart:
  - **Already in the cart:** clicking "Add to Crate" updates your cart to whatever quantity is shown — it does not add on top.
  - **Not in the cart yet:** clicking "Add to Crate" adds the selected quantity as a fresh item, as normal.
